    ! The Moksh Life !


    known for manufacturing and distributing of Swarna Champa Incense Sticks and apart from that we are also engaged in manufacturing, supplying, trading, wholesaling, distributing and exporting a wide range of Lucky Kewda Incense Sticks, Lavender Incense Sticks, Gulab Incense Sticks, Mogra Incense Sticks, Rose Incense Sticks, Jasmine Incense Sticks, Oriental Musk Incense Sticks, Scented Incense Sticks and many more. The agarbatti is used for worship in temples, homes and many more places. The incense stick is made using a bamboo stick which is coated with blend of aromatic substance and through a manual process of hand rolling and induction of aromatic oil.
